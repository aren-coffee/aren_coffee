— Welcome

A coffee space for the Arctic

AREN Coffee is a proposed grab-and-go café in Resolute, Nunavut. Designed for quick, high-quality coffee, hot beverages, and light snacks — with minimal seating and maximum warmth.

Our mission: offer a warm, accessible café experience that supports social connection in a remote Arctic town. Compact, energy-efficient, and built for Arctic conditions.

Designer role: Concept, branding & operational guidance. Local partners implement & operate.

☕❄️

Why Resolute?

Small population (~200) with limited food options. Harsh climate demands warm drinks. Ideal for residents, researchers, and tourists. Grab-and-go format = simple, low-risk, high community value.
